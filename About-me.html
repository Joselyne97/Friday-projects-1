I am dressed for business and I am a courageous girl and a hard worker, as a leader I took the initiative of teaching and training people, especially women who are not competitive to the local market, to transform the abandoned waste materials like plastic bottles, old clothes and other disposal materials into ornamental materials to be used in interior design as an environmental friendly manner. We envision a world where setting up a creative recycling company and designing projects that will be among the master mind and spear header of environmental protection will address the environmental issues we are now facing today and introduce projects that bring transformative changes in our nation and on the global scale in terms of environmental protection.
